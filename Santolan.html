Santolan

The result is creamy, sour, and lightly spicy; a unique take on fruit-based cooking in Bicol food culture.


Ingredients

Base:Star Ingredient: Whole or chopped Pili nuts.
Mixture: Sweetened beans (munggo, kidney beans), macapuno (coconut sport), nata de coco, kaong (sugar palm fruit), ube halaya, and langka (jackfruit). White or brown sugar, a small amount of water, and sometimes a touch of butter or vanilla for richness.

Procedure

1. Sauté garlic, onions, and minced pork.

2. Mix in grated santol and shrimp paste.

3. Add coconut milk and sliced chilies.

4. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally.

5. Wait until the liquid reduces and the oil separates.

Recipe Info

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 2–4
Difficulty: Easy
