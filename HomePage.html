Kinalas

Tender Pork Brain Gravy and Yellow Noodles

Noodle Soup

A well-known street staple in Naga City, kinalas is a hearty noodle soup topped with slow-cooked shredded beef and a thick , savory gravy made from the broth’s reduced stock. Market it as a local alternative to ramen or chicken mami that delivers the same satisfaction at a lower cost.
