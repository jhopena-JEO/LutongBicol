Pinangat

Pinangat is similar to laing but uses whole taro leaves wrapped around fish, shrimp, or meat, tied neatly, then simmered in coconut milk.


Ingredients

Base:Star Ingredient: Whole or chopped Pili nuts.
Mixture: Sweetened beans (munggo, kidney beans), macapuno (coconut sport), nata de coco, kaong (sugar palm fruit), ube halaya, and langka (jackfruit). White or brown sugar, a small amount of water, and sometimes a touch of butter or vanilla for richness.

Procedure

1. Combine shredded coconut, ginger, and shrimp paste.

2. Place filling inside layered gabi leaves and tie securely.

3. Arrange pouches in a pot over a bed of lemongrass.

4. Pour coconut milk and simmer until the leaves are tender.

5. Cook until the sauce is thick and oily.

Recipe Info

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 2–4
Difficulty: Easy
