Pili Nut Brittle

The pili nut, one of Bicol’s most treasured products, is known for its buttery texture and rich flavor.


Ingredients

Base:Star Ingredient: Whole or chopped Pili nuts.
Mixture: Sweetened beans (munggo, kidney beans), macapuno (coconut sport), nata de coco, kaong (sugar palm fruit), ube halaya, and langka (jackfruit). White or brown sugar, a small amount of water, and sometimes a touch of butter or vanilla for richness.

Procedure

1. Roast pili nuts until crunchy and golden.

2. Heat sugar and water until it turns into amber caramel.

3. Stir the toasted nuts into the hot syrup.

4. Pour the mixture onto a greased flat surface.

5. Let it harden completely, then break into pieces.

Recipe Info

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 2–4
Difficulty: Easy
